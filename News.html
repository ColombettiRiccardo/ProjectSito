Call for papers for a special issue on Sustainable Water and Energy Solutions

Call for Papers

NRF calls for papers for a special issue on Sustainable Water and Energy Solutions, to be published in November 2022. The issue will promote understanding of the technology-policy nexus and enhance global awareness of ongoing integrated efforts on water and energy, as well as interlinkages with other SDGs.


For more information, please see the full Call for Papers


The deadline for submitting articles is 30th June 2022.
